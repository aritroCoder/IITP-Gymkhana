Students' Gymkhana, IIT Patna

The Students' Gymkhana of IIT Patna (established in 2008) is the governing council of students consisting of the elected student representatives working for the over-all well being and holistic development of students in the institute.

Know More

About Student's Gymkhana

The Students' Gymkhana at IIT Patna is a dynamic council that leads the charge in promoting leadership, talent, and co-curricular activities among students. With elected representatives from each hostel, the Gymkhana coordinates various events and activities to bring out the best in every student.
Sports and cultural events are a cornerstone of campus life, and the Gymkhana is at the forefront of organizing inter-hostel and inter-department competitions that bring out healthy competition, relaxation, and camaraderie among participants. The blending of different cultures adds a unique character to the cultural events organized throughout the year.
To make learning an enjoyable experience, the Gymkhana organizes workshops, seminars, and technical activities. The various clubs and groups at IIT Patna come together to organize the big fests, such as ANWESHA, INFINITO, CELESTA, REVERBERANCE, etc. These fests are a testament to the creativity and talent of the students and have become an integral part of the IITP legacy.

In short, the Students' Gymkhana of IIT Patna is a vital force that shapes the holistic development of students and creates a vibrant and inclusive community of future leaders. Its efforts have helped to create an environment where students can thrive and reach their full potential, both academically and personally.

HOOT

×

“There is nothing like Oratory, it can turn a commoner into a king.”
The House of Oratory Talents, endearingly called HOOT, is the official speaking arts society of IIT Patna. We aim to provide an accessible and dynamic platform where the art of effective communication takes flight. As the beacon of eloquence and persuasion, Hoot empowers individuals to refine their public speaking skills, articulate their ideas, and captivate audiences with their words. Within the nurturing environment of Hoot, members engage in thought-provoking debates, enthralling extempore sessions, and engaging storytelling activities. Through regular workshops and constructive feedback, Hoot cultivates confident speakers who can inspire, influence, and lead. Join Hoot and unlock the power of oratory excellence, where every word catalyses change.
