“There is nothing like Oratory, it can turn a commoner into a king.”
The House of Oratory Talents, endearingly called HOOT, is the official speaking arts society of IIT Patna. We aim to provide an accessible and dynamic platform where the art of effective communication takes flight. As the beacon of eloquence and persuasion, Hoot empowers individuals to refine their public speaking skills, articulate their ideas, and captivate audiences with their words. Within the nurturing environment of Hoot, members engage in thought-provoking debates, enthralling extempore sessions, and engaging storytelling activities. Through regular workshops and constructive feedback, Hoot cultivates confident speakers who can inspire, influence, and lead. Join Hoot and unlock the power of oratory excellence, where every word catalyses change.
