Students' Gymkhana, IIT Patna

The Students' Gymkhana of IIT Patna (established in 2008) is the governing council of students consisting of the elected student representatives working for the over-all well being and holistic development of students in the institute.

Know More

About Us

The Students' Gymkhana at IIT Patna is a dynamic council that leads the charge in promoting leadership, talent, and co-curricular activities among students. With elected representatives from each hostel, the Gymkhana coordinates various events and activities to bring out the best in every student. Sports and cultural events are a cornerstone of campus life, and the Gymkhana is at the forefront of organizing inter-hostel and inter-department competitions that bring out healthy competition, relaxation, and camaraderie among participants. The blending of different cultures adds a unique character to the cultural events organized throughout the year.

To make learning an enjoyable experience, the Gymkhana organizes workshops, seminars, and technical activities. The various clubs and groups at IIT Patna come together to organize the big fests, such as ANWESHA, INFINITO, CELESTA, REVERBERANCE, etc. These fests are a testament to the creativity and talent of the students and have become an integral part of the IITP legacy. In short, the Students' Gymkhana of IIT Patna is a vital force that shapes the holistic development of students and creates a vibrant and inclusive community of future leaders. Its efforts have helped to create an environment where students can thrive and reach their full potential, both academically and personally.

“There is nothing like Oratory, it can turn a commoner into a king.”
The House of Oratory Talents, endearingly called HOOT, is the official speaking arts society of IIT Patna. We aim to provide an accessible and dynamic platform where the art of effective communication takes flight. As the beacon of eloquence and persuasion, Hoot empowers individuals to refine their public speaking skills, articulate their ideas, and captivate audiences with their words. Within the nurturing environment of Hoot, members engage in thought-provoking debates, enthralling extempore sessions, and engaging storytelling activities. Through regular workshops and constructive feedback, Hoot cultivates confident speakers who can inspire, influence, and lead. Join Hoot and unlock the power of oratory excellence, where every word catalyses change.

Hexachrome

×

A fairly new addition to the HoSCA umbrella, Hexachrome is the official Puzzle-solving Club of IIT Patna. It is a welcoming space where members can explore a wide variety of puzzles, ranging from traditional Crosswords, Scrabble and Sudoku to much more complicated and fancy Killer Sudoku, Cryptic Crosswords, and even the Rubik’s Cube. Puzzle-solving has many benefits, including cognitive development, stress relief, and improved critical thinking skills. The club allows students to engage in a fun and challenging activity while developing these skills. It is an effective way of reducing stress and anxiety and is known to impact the mental well-being of partakers positively. Whether you are an ardent puzzle enthusiast or simply looking to exercise your brain and meet like-minded individuals, the Puzzle Solving Club offers an engaging and intellectually stimulating community. Join us today and embark on a journey of mind-bending challenges, endless curiosity, and the sheer joy of puzzle-solving.

We will continue the legacy of unconditionally guiding fellow students of the IIT Patna fraternity across the various domains of Computer Science.

AP Club

Astronomy and Particle Physics (AP) Club is a team of enthusiastic people who share a common goal of exploring, studying, and experimenting with a wide variety of Astronomy and Particle Physics related topics, facts, and new discoveries. We have been trying to make people aware of scintillating stuff right from the nano-level world to the vastness of the cosmos!

SCME

×

SCME was founded with the motive of engaging and encouraging Mechanical Engineering discipline related activities in IIT Patna. Since its inception, SCME has been conducting events, lectures and workshops round the year for the benefit of mechanical engineering students.

MATES

×

MaTES is a combined step of students and faculty of IIT PATNA to create an environment of inquisitiveness about metallurgy and materials through various interesting events, informative workshops and inquisitive guest lectures. Our organization's aim is to promote creativity, potency, morals in students and boost awareness about materials in the society.

ChESSx

×

ChESSx aims at providing the needed support to produce the best chemical engineering graduates of tomorrow. It aims at making the students familiar with the scientific and industrial know-how and well equipping them to handle real-world problems so that they can better adapt themselves at their workplace once they graduate.

Sparkonics

×

Sparkonics is the Electrical engineering society of IIT Patna which aims to make students aware and better equipped to handle real world problems related to Electrical engineering and related interdisciplinary fields by the means of projects and various workshops and guest lectures.

Robocon

×

Team Phoenix IIT Patna is the official robotics contingent of IIT Patna in ABU Robocon, Asia's Largest Robotics contest. We are a passionate group of students seeking like-minded individuals to join us. Our team comprises skilled members in computer vision, PID control, circuit design, app development, microcontroller programming, and mechanism design. Starting from 2023, we will also participate in the prestigious MathWorks Simulation contest alongside Robocon. We offer diverse learning opportunities in robotics through workshops, seminars, hackathons, and mentorship programs for students interested in pursuing their own robotics projects.
